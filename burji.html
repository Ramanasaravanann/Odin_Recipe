Tomato Egg Bhurji (Indian-Style Scrambled Eggs)

Description

A quick and tasty protein-rich dish made with eggs, tomato, and spices. Perfect for breakfast or a light meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until soft.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  3. Add turmeric and salt, mix well
  4. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and stir continuously.
  5. Cook until the eggs are scrambled and fully cooked.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with bread or chapati
