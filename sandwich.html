Veg Sandwich

Description:

A quick, no-cook, healthy sandwich ideal for snacks or a light meal. Customizable with your favorite veggies.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (brown or white)
  • 1 small cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 small tomato (sliced)
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise or butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional: Cheese slice, green chutney

Steps:

  1. Spread butter or mayonnaise on each bread slice.
  2. Layer cucumber, tomato, and onion slices on one side of the bread.
  3. Sprinkle a little salt and pepper.
  4. Add cheese or chutney if desired.
  5. Cover with another bread slice and press gently.
  6. Cut into halves and serve fresh.
