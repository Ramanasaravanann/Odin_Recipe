Curd Rice

Description

A refreshing South Indian comfort food made with yogurt and rice. Great for digestion and easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp grated carrot (optional)
  • Few curry leaves
  • Few coriander leaves (chopped)

Step:

  1. In a bowl, mix the cooked rice and curd until well combined.
  2. Add salt and carrot, mix gently.
  3. Heat oil in a small pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add curry leaves and pour the tempering over the rice.
  5. Mix everything gently and garnish with coriander leaves.
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
